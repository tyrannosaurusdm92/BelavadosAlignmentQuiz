What Is The Belavadös Alignment System?

The Belavadös Alignment System rejects the idea that morality can be reduced to simplistic labels such as “good,” “evil,” “lawful,” or “chaotic.” Instead, identity is treated as a living reflection of accumulated behavior, sacrifice, loyalty, ambition, and consequence.

Every character is evaluated across four independent moral axes:

Altruism

Measures selflessness versus selfish ambition.

Lawfulness

Measures devotion to order, structure, and discipline.

Cooperation

Measures loyalty, teamwork, and social unity.

Honor

Measures integrity, honesty, and moral consistency.

Your alignment is not predetermined. It evolves through your decisions, relationships, convictions, betrayals, and sacrifices.
