Belavadös System

Merged from both solar-system designs: this version keeps the richer 360° navigation, click-to-fly objects, panels, nebulae, debris fields, moons, and all docx-matched non-constellation content, while using the planet and sun size logic from solar_system.html. This revision further widens the rendered orbit spacing without changing the listed kilometer/AU distances, adds the Eiršu'neth Comet as a safe, inclined, long-period Vishtecu–Hyperu family comet, and updates the Belavadös lunar system with the corrected nested moon paths and much smaller true-scale moon rendering based on Belavadös as an Earth-sized world.

Drag to rotate 360°, hold Shift while dragging for slower precision rotation, scroll to zoom, and click any rendered asset to fly toward it.

Left-drag = rotate 360° • Shift + left-drag = precision rotation • Wheel = zoom • Click asset = fly/zoom • Nebulae and debris regions drift, planets orbit on wider non-flat inclined paths; Eiršu'neth Comet follows a luminous 18° inclined safe orbit
