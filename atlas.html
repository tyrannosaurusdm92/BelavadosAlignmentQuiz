Pantheon

The Belavadös pantheon is not a comforting roll call of distant protectors. These imprisoned gods are ancient predatory creators: beautiful, uncanny, hungry, and still powerful enough to reach through dreams, worship, ichor, prison rites, and divine dependency. Their containment protects civilization, but it also keeps the world chained to the same horrors it tried to overthrow.
