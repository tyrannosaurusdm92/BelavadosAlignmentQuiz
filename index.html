Belavadös Alignment System

A living alignment system for your homebrew world

Belavadös does not treat alignment like a fixed label. It tracks how a character moves through four independent moral axes: Altruism, Lawfulness, Cooperation, and Honor. The result is a character profile that can shift with decisions, pressure, sacrifice, loyalty, and survival.

Core Rules
Each axis ranges from 0 to 3000, with 1500 as neutral.

Higher or lower values reflect consistent behavior over time, not a one-time impulse.

What the axes mean
Altruism

Self-serving ↔ altruistic. Measures compassion, sacrifice, and exploitation.

Lawfulness

Chaotic ↔ lawful. Measures obedience, order, duty, and rebellion.

Cooperation

Individualistic ↔ cooperative. Measures trust, loyalty, and teamwork.

Honor

Dishonorable ↔ honorable. Measures honesty, restraint, and integrity.
