Monk Ki / Focus Points

For 2014 5e monk Ki tracking, adaptable for homebrew Focus/Discipline points

How to Track

In the 2014 5e monk rules, Ki / Focus points become available at monk level 2. Spent Ki / Focus returns after a short or long rest, provided the monk spends at least 30 minutes meditating during the rest. Ki / Focus save DC is normally 8 + proficiency bonus + Wisdom modifier.

Common Ki / Focus Uses

Flurry of Blows

After taking the Attack action, spend 1 point to make two unarmed strikes as a bonus action.

Patient Defense

Spend 1 point to take Dodge as a bonus action.

Step of the Wind

Spend 1 point to take Dash or Disengage as a bonus action; jump distance doubles for the turn.

Stunning Strike

When you hit with a melee weapon attack, spend 1 point to force a Constitution save against being stunned until your next turn.
